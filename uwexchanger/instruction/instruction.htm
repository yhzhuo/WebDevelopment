How to use UWexchanger?

Firstly, you need to register at http://www.uwexchanger.com/register.php. Note, you MUST use your UW email address to register it. We recommend you fill in the information about what courses you need , and what courses you don’t need in the two tables below. It will make you feel easier to use UWexchanger in the future. Note, never type in SLN!!! The reason is that just like schedule finder of UW, our system never use SLN to match courses. Additionally, you'd better also type in your current situation and contact information (Phone Number Recommended!!!) below these two tables, it will help other users find you quickly

When you register it successfully, the page will jump to the swapping control panel.

If there is already someone who matches your need that recorded in our system, you can see their whole information, including what they can provide you and what you should provide them and their contact information. Then you can contact with them and get the courses you need. (1)

If you cannot find someone who matches your need, don’t feel said, just click on the refresh button (5) and see if there are someone who matches, because someone who matches your need may probably post their information just after you. You can also close the webpage and open it again after few minutes. Sometimes, others will contact you before you see the webpage again.

When you get the courses you need, you’d better delete your request about these courses. If others get your courses, you should also delete your information about "you have but don’t need" those courses. Why, because if you don’t do like that, some people may bother you for the courses you have already given to others. If you hope to delete a request, just check the check box on the left side of the course information (3) .Besides that, if you have some new request for some courses or you begin to unlike another course you have, you can also add the new information.

  Now, you may ask, how to add and delete the information about courses? It's easy! Do you remember how you add and drop courses in UW courses registration page? Just choose the courses you hope to drop, and type in the SLN of the courses you hope to register, then click on the button “Update Schedule” right? In UWexchanger, you do the same thing. The only difference is that what you type in is not SLN, but the courses’ Curriculum Abbreviation, Number and Section in the places of “Add the courses I need” or “Add the courses I don’t need” that correspond to them. For example for “CSE 142 A” the Curriculum Abbreviation is CSE, the Number is 142, the Section is A.

There are two important things you need to know!!! First, when you type in your new courses requests, or check on the requests that you hope to delete, they haven't been added or deleted. You need to click on the button "Change Courses!!!" (7) that below two tables. Then the system can do all the jobs for you.



Secondly If you do not need to type in the specific section of a course you need, don't type! The reason is that our system will try to only present you others course request information that not only relate to the specific courses but also related to the specific section, which sometimes will be very rare. 

However, updating your contact information is a little different. You need to write these in the "My situation & how to contact me:" box, and then click the "Change Situation" button below it.

Useful tips to use UWexchanger

1: Phone number is the best contact method, because you and your exchanging partner can directly talk in phone and drop, and add, which is very convenient.
2: During the exchanging process, please all record the SLNs first then do the adding and dropping, because you should do this as quickly as possible, or other students who are also looking for these courses may rub the places that you deserve to get.
3: Don’t type the courses information too specific or too general. Too general information may let the system provide some irrelevant people to you, whereas too specific information may let the system ignore the people who may fit in your demand. So, if you hope to get “CSE 142 A”, you’d better type in “CSE 142”, which may let the system show people who can provide you “CSE 142 B”. Then, you may find that “CSE 142 B” also fit in your demand.
4: Our system will show you up to 20 people in one page, which means that some valuable information may hide in the second or third or other pages. So, you can click the green padding "Show more" below the list of the courses request information to see more results. However, if it changes into gary padding with words "No more!!!", all results are presented in your browser. One important thing is that the people in the bottom place of a page or the people in the back pages are always relatively irrelevant to your demand or don’t consider their courses in the exchanger for long time.
5: If you cannot find the people who match you for a long time but still don’t give up, you can try to update the information you put in our system a little. For example add a semicolon in your contact information, and so on. This method is used to make your information tend to appear in the front pages, but remember, do NOT always do like this, or others may bothered by you.
6: Sometimes, you just hope to get the courses you need but do not to give up any of your courses. In this situation, you can still leave the "what you have but don't need" blank. However, if you do like this, probably you cannot get the courses you need.
